How do you ensure the freshness of your seafood?

All our seafood is sourced daily from local fishing communities. We use specialized cooling technology during transportation and deliver within hours of catch to ensure maximum freshness.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, online banking transfers, e-wallets (Touch 'n Go, GrabPay, Boost), and cash on delivery for orders under RM300.

Do you offer bulk or wholesale orders?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk and wholesale orders. Please contact our wholesale department at wholesale@oceanfresh.my or call +60 3 1234 5679 for more information.
